Dominant Consciousness

Embodied cognition, survival instinct, no formal symbolic systems.

Key Notes

Emergence of Homo sapiens. Post–ice age genetic bottleneck. Foundations of sensory awareness.

Dominant Consciousness

Animism, shamanic ritual, symbolic externalization.

Burial rites, ochre, Venus figurines. Löwenmensch sculpture. First mythic representations.

Key Notes

Rise of agriculture. Canaanite theology. Tribe of Dan. Temple systems and sacred calendars.
